Our Founder

Meet Jun, whose vision and expertise form the foundation of our supply chain solutions.

Jun Lee - Founder 

With 18 years of dedicated experience in the health supplement industry, Jun founded SuppBridge to address the critical gaps in global supply chains. Based in Changsha, his expertise spans the entire product lifecycle from concept development to market distribution.

Industry Leadership

Jun has held senior positions as Supply Chain Director and Marketing Director for multiple international health supplement brands, gaining invaluable insights into global market dynamics and operational challenges.

Entrepreneurial Vision

His e-commerce entrepreneurship journey equipped him with the ability to identify market opportunities and develop innovative business models that bridge production and consumer demands.

Global Network Builder

Jun has cultivated an extensive network of reliable supply chain resources spanning China, Australia, New Zealand, the United States, and Europe, including raw material suppliers, manufacturing facilities with diverse formulation capabilities, third-party testing laboratories, and international logistics teams.

Problem-Solving Expertise

Jun's greatest strength lies in developing flexible solutions to complex challenges, whether navigating regulatory requirements, optimizing costs without compromising quality, or creating alternative supply routes during disruptions.

About SuppBridge

Founded by Jun, SuppBridge was created to address the critical challenges in the global health supplement supply chain. Our strength lies in our extensive network and flexible approach to problem-solving.

Our Global Network

We maintain reliable supply chain resources across China, Australia, New Zealand, the United States, and Europe. This includes raw material suppliers, manufacturing facilities with diverse formulation capabilities, third-party testing laboratories, and international logistics teams.

Our Philosophy

We believe in building long-term partnerships based on trust, transparency, and results. Our approach combines global reach with local expertise to create supply chain solutions that adapt to your specific needs.

Our Mission

To simplify the complexities of global health supplement supply chains, enabling our clients to focus on what they do best – creating products that improve people's health and well-being.
